Our Story

WAYS® began with two best friends—Maria (Mexican) & Julia (Dutch) — who met while studying in Rotterdam back in 2017. Four years ago, we moved to Amsterdam to start our careers — and quickly bonded over our passion for wellness, active lifestyle, and the little rituals that help us feel our best from the inside out. Over the years, as we dove deeper into health, supplementation, and performance optimization, we noticed something missing: clean, effective electrolytes that fit seamlessly into daily life—especially in the Netherlands. We experienced firsthand how powerful electrolytes can be— not just for workouts, but for focus, mood, energy, and recovery. But the options we found were limited and mostly filled with sugar, artificial ingredients, inconvenient packaging, or marketed only to athletes.

So we created WAYS®: a lifestyle hydration brand designed for real people, real routines, and real results.
